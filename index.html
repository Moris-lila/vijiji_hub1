About Us

At Vijiji Hub, our goal is to ensure we transform rural innovation and entrepreneurial ecosystem for achieving SDGs. We strongly believe that achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) will require the support of entrepreneurs. Rural innovations and start-ups have a key role to play.


Empowering diverse communities, Vijiji Hub fosters local talent and creativity, bridging the gap between urban and rural aspirations. Through mentorship and collaboration, it cultivates resilient, socially responsible entrepreneurs, sparking positive change.

What We Do

  • 1

    Supporting Visionary Entrepreneurs

    Vijiji Hub identifies and supports the unseen visionary entrepreneurs (youth and women), providing vital support and resources for sustainable innovations that positively impact urban slums and rural areas.

  • 2

    Business Incubation and start-up training

    Vijiji Hub serves as a vibrant start-up community incubator, creating opportunities for collaboration; as well as hands-on training and mentorship delivered by experts.

  • 3

    Harnessing technology to ignite rapid rural start-up business

    Vijiji Hub partners with Tech companies to provide digital solutions, increasing digital awareness and literacy, making e-commerce a fundamental growth driver for rural start-ups to celebrate innovation and entrepreneurship.

  • 4

    Linking Diaspora community to promote rural start-up innovation

    Vijiji Hub provides the space and networking platform that enables rural startups to access diaspora talent, knowledge and resources to grow their business.

The Transformative Power of rural Entrepreneurship

Vijiji Hub's journey from urban slums to remote villages exemplifies the the Transformative Power of rural Entrepreneurship . In every challenge lies an opportunity for innovation, and through collaboration, resilience, and visionary leadership, we can build a future where sustainable businesses drive positive change for all

Vijiji Hub:

Our goal is to see urban slums and rural areas rise above the technological gaps to become a centre of attraction for the global startups: o Harnessing the potential for Startups to thrive: We do this by identifying and analyzing triggers for innovation in rural areas. o Partnering with university students to steer rural innovation more effectively: we do this by creating opportunities for students and youth to interact with diverse startups. o Harnessing the power of technology improvements to propel rural startup forward. o Connecting rural innovation actors to diaspora community and investors to ensure the foster a global mindset: We do this through community-led meet-up groups (#ViSIT) that build vibrant global networks.

Main Focus

From urban slums to remote villages (rural areas), Vijiji Hub is driving an inclusive and impact-driven model for sustainable innovative entrepreneurship. Our main goal is to support start-ups! From urban slums to remote villages, we identify the unseen innovators and link them to potential investors, donors, organizations/mentors, and networks. We endeavor to bring incubation and business support close to our youth/community to ensure their ideas thrive, develop skills and networks and give these unemployed and forgotten groups a chance to participate and be seen by the world.
